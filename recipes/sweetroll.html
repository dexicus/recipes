Sweetroll

The iconic sweet treat loved by all across Tamriel!

Crisp and crunchy on the outside, moist and soft on the inside. This is a truly legendary treat!

Here is the sweetest sweetroll recipe you'll ever need!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large bundt tin (or 2 smaller ones)
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1 cup maple syrup or honey
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 60g butter, melted
  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped
  • 3 tsp cinnamon powder

For the glaze:

  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped

Steps:

  1. Preheat over to 200C/392F
  2. Grease your bundt tin well with butter
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine all the baking ingredients and mix well
  4. Pour into the bundt tin(s) and bake for 45 minutes, or until brown and risen
  5. It should be firm and crusty on the outside
  6. Flip onto a wire rack to cool before transferring to a plate

For the glaze:

  1. Combine the melted butter, sugar, and vanilla in a small bowl
  2. Whip until well blended
  3. Drizzle over the top of your sweetroll and wait til icing has hardened before eating
